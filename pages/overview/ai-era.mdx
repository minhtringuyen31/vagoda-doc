The Era of GenAI marks a modern period with significant advancements in artificial intelligence. GenAI, once an abstract concept
in computer science, has become indispensable in daily life. It permeates almost all fields, from science, technology, and economics
to society, culture, and education, bringing unprecedented changes.

## GenAI Impact

### Economy

GenAI is transforming the global economy by creating new industries and optimizing production processes. It analyzes large datasets,
predicts market trends, and supports business decision-making, enhancing operational efficiency, reducing costs, and increasing competitiveness.

## Limitations of GenAI

Despite its advancements, Generative AI faces limitations. Poor or biased training data can lead to inaccurate results. Reliability
remains a challenge, especially in high-precision fields like healthcare and law. Ethical and security concerns arise as GenAI can
create fake information or infringe on privacy. Automation with GenAI also poses unemployment risks, particularly in IT and customer
service jobs.

## Future Potential

Generative AI holds vast potential for expanding and improving existing applications. It can create new products and services, improve
production processes, and enhance user experiences. Current research focuses on improving model accuracy and reliability, reducing data
bias, and exploring synergies between GenAI and technologies like IoT and blockchain to develop innovative solutions.
