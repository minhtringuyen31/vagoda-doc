# Scope & Vision

The expected product will be an e-commerce platform with high perfection in terms of user interface and user experience while meeting all user needs.
The system comprises of a web platform for buyers and a web platform for sellers with complete interface and functions.

The websites integrate Machine Learning and GenAI into a number of functions, optimize user experience and bring efficiency to users' shopping and trading. 
The system ensures to meet the requirements for safe data management and storage, high security, sufficient load capacity, expansion ability, etc. 

Our team carefully implements and revises features from the user's perspective to provide the most comfortable, meaningful experience in shopping and trading.
